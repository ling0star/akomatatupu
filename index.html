Applications for the 2026 Cohort Are Now Open

You're in the right place to begin your journey with the Teach First NZ Programme. Join a community of Kairapu — change-makers working for educational equity and a more just Aotearoa. We're excited to learn more about your passion for making a difference through teaching.

What to Expect

  • Check your eligibility:

    Ensure you meet the basic requirements for the programme before proceeding with your application.

  • Share Your Story:

    Tell us about your background, experiences, and what drives you to create social change through education. You'll also provide detail about your education and experience to inform us as to which subjects you might be able to teach.

  • After You Apply:

    If successful, you’ll be invited to a phone or Zoom interview. From there, selected candidates attend our one-day Ako Centre, which includes a pepeha, interview, group activity, teaching sample, and reflection. Offers to join the programme follow soon after.

    The journey begins with a seven-week Summer Intensive starting mid-November, before placement in a partner secondary school the following year.

Questions?

Contact us at apply@teachfirstnz.org or call 0800 TO LEAD.

Commitment to Kaupapa

Teach First NZ's mission (kaupapa) is to address educational inequity by placing exceptional graduates and professionals in low-decile secondary schools to teach for two years. Our programme aims to develop future leaders who are committed to making a lasting impact on the lives of young people.
